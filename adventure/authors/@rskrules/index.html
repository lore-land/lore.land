Hemera Nyx

Space Adventurer Hemera Nyx blasts off across the cosmos in her reliable spaceship, the Sparrow. Bereft of memory, our hero scours the deep reaches of outer space in search of a way home.

Hostile alien worlds. Strange lifeforms both friendly and not. Unstoppable intergalactic empires. Prison planets made of metal and poison. Will Hemera survive the galaxy of the future? Or will the secrets of her past find her in the cold, dark void?
