Chapter 03: Boof and the Fool's Paradox

Boof stood at the edge of the Fool's Paradox, a place where truth and jest twisted together, impossible to untangle.

"To move forward, you must go back," the paradox whispered, echoing the Fool’s riddles. "To catch the Fool, you must first be caught."

"You see, Boof, you are always moving, but never arriving," the Fool's voice teased. "The closer you get, the further away I am."

The Enigma of the Paradox

The Fool's Paradox bent reality itself. Boof’s every step felt like progress, yet no distance was crossed. The boonberries glowed with a mischievous light, flickering between reality and mirage.

Boof laughed. The paradox was no puzzle to solve, but a lesson to learn. "To chase is to never catch," Boof realized, but even as they did, the Fool’s laughter echoed through the twisted grove.
